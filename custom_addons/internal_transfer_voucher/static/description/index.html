Internal Transfers & Payment Vouchers

This module enhances Odoo Accounting by restoring classic internal accounting operations that were simplified in Odoo 18.

Key Features

  • Internal transfers between Bank and Cash journals
  • Outbound payment vouchers (Cash / Cheque)
  • Single journal entry posting (Odoo-native)
  • Optional bank fees with VAT handling
  • Clean Draft → Posted → Cancel workflows
  • Professional printable vouchers
  • Bilingual PDF reports (English / Arabic)
  • Arabic amount in words

Payment Voucher

Designed for petty cash and manual payments, the Payment Voucher provides a clean and accountant-friendly form with a professional printable layout.

Internal Transfers

Internal Transfers allow controlled movement between bank and cash journals, including optional bank fees and VAT, while keeping accounting entries clean and balanced.

Designed For

  • Middle East accounting practices
  • Companies migrating from Odoo 16 / 17 to Odoo 18
  • Users who require classic vouchers instead of simplified UI flows

Compatibility

Fully compatible with Odoo 18. No external dependencies.
